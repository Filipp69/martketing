who we are?

Combining our proven experience, confirmed by special projects, exhibitions, events, teaching and awards in the creative area, working with the global brands and contemporary artists, we have developed an exceptional formula for brand intercommunion with the audience.

Martketing agency makes the dialogue between contemporary art and brand possible based on the idea — the most relevant and inexhaustible tool for working with the audience on the modern market, overflowing with a variety of images and visual forms.

Martketing aimed to offer outstanding solutions for brand positioning in the market: loud statements and unique opportunities for the audience to meet their own reflection in the brand concept through art.

what we offer?

Working with the synthesis of marketing and contemporary art, we offer our clients elegant, conceptual, unique business scenario solving.We help brands use relevant tools in creating contact with the audience. Art is a tool for self-discovery, it opens up something new for both the audience and the brand itself at the level of core values.

The result of collaboration can be not only a product or a one-time project, but also a long-term series of integrations consisting of different formats. Special projects, events, site-specific art, photo and video campaigns, spaces, social projects — synthesis of contemporary art and marketing has no boundaries. Working with MARTKETING, the brand becomes a part of the artistic research and process of creating a unique project.

We offer full service of project actualization from the idea to all project production management.
